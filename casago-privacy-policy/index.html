Privacy Policy for Casago

Last updated:

1. Introduction

Casago (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a ride-request and driver-dispatch application which allows riders to request transportation and enables drivers to accept and complete rides.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the Casago mobile application.

By using Casago, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone number
  • Pickup and destination addresses
  • Ride details (date, time, price, distance, ETA)

2.2 Driver Information

  • Driver name
  • Phone number
  • Vehicle details (model, plate)
  • Real-time GPS location (only when sharing is enabled)

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (for address autocomplete and distance calculations)

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1 To Provide Ride Services

  • Find the nearest available driver
  • Calculate distance, duration, and ETA
  • Notify drivers and riders of ride status
  • Enable driver reply flow (YES / PICKEDUP / DONE)
  • Send rider notifications

3.2 Communications

We send SMS notifications through trusted providers (such as Twilio or Infobip) for ride updates.

3.3 Payments (If enabled)

Payment data is processed securely by Stripe. We do not store credit card information.

3.4 Security and Fraud Prevention

We may use your data to detect and prevent suspicious activity.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase: Database and backend storage
  • Google Maps APIs: Geocoding, Distance Matrix, Autocomplete
  • Twilio / Infobip: SMS delivery
  • Stripe (optional): Secure payment processing

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We only share data with:

  • Drivers (pickup location and ride info)
  • SMS providers to deliver transactional messages
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Google Maps APIs

6. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase with encrypted connections to store your data securely.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encryption and access control.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

You may request deletion of your data at any time.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions

For requests, contact: your-email@example.com

9. Children’s Privacy

Casago is not intended for individuals under 18 years old.
